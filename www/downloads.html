ESLP (stanalone app), OS: Windows, x64 only
Latest version: 0.34 (April 30, 2022)

Download the latest version (zip archive, 250 Kb).
See version history to download older release (project homepage).

A program for finding solutions to Diophantine equations of the form k.m.n. Searches for the first primitive solutions, starting from the optionally specified values of the leading coefficients. Features and known issues:

  • Equation degree is limited to 20 (valid values are 1-20).
  • The total number of equation coefficients is limited to 160.
  • Max. coefficient values are limited to 128-bit arithmetic.
  • Low performance for equations of even powers >4.
  • Multi-threaded computation.
